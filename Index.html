Welcome to Varad Seeds India Pvt. Ltd.

Varad Seeds is engaged in the production, processing and marketing of vegetable seeds for growers and trading partners across India and overseas.

Focused on Grower Success

We work closely with growers, breeders and channel partners to supply seed lots that are field-tested for vigour, yield and quality. Our portfolio covers major vegetable crops suitable for different regions and seasons.

With robust quality systems from intake to packing, Varad Seeds aims to deliver uniform and reliable performance in every bag. We believe in transparent business practices, timely communication and long-term relationships.
